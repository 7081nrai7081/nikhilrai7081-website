FAQ

Common questions

What kind of organizations do you work with?

Founders, growth teams and small-to-mid-sized businesses that want to build partnerships, generate leads and scale their operations more systematically.

How do engagements usually work?

It depends on the need — from focused projects (like building a lead-gen system or launching a partnership program) to ongoing advisory. We scope it together on a first call.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. I work with organizations remotely and collaborate over the tools your team already uses.

What's the difference between partnerships and business development?

Business development is the broad work of finding and creating growth opportunities; strategic partnerships are one high-leverage channel within it — building relationships with other organizations that share your audience.

Can you help with just one area?

Absolutely. You can pick a single service — say, a lead-gen system or one partnership program — or combine several into an end-to-end growth engine. We start with whatever moves your numbers most.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope — a one-off project is priced differently from ongoing advisory. I'll give you a clear quote after our first call, once we both know what you actually need.

How soon will I see results?

It depends on the work. Some automations and processes pay off within weeks; partnerships and pipeline-building compound over months. I'll be honest on the first call about a realistic timeline for your situation.

How do we get started?

Book a free 30-minute call. We'll talk through your goals and figure out whether and how I can help.
