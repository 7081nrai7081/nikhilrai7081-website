What kind of organizations do you work with?

Founders, growth teams and small-to-mid-sized businesses that want to build partnerships, generate leads and scale their operations more systematically.

How do engagements usually work?

It depends on the need — from focused projects (like building a lead-gen system or launching a partnership program) to ongoing advisory. We scope it together on a first call.

What's the difference between partnerships and business development?

Business development is the broad work of finding and creating growth opportunities; strategic partnerships are one high-leverage channel within it — building relationships with other organizations that share your audience.
