About

Who I Am

I build and scale partnerships in the U.S. home-seller-leads space — onboarding marketplaces, brokerages, referral networks and direct lead buyers into a reliable, TCPA-compliant seller supply.

My work spans partner sourcing and onboarding, commercial structuring (CPL, pay-per-call, rev-share), real-time integration and delivery (ping-post / host-post, API, webhooks), and quality and compliance.

I believe sustainable growth comes from combining strong relationships, efficient systems and consistent execution — proven with a short pilot, then scaled nationwide with transparent reporting.
